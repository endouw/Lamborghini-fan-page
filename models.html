Aventador

The Lamborghini Aventador is a mid-engine design sports car produced by the Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini. In keeping with Lamborghini tradition, the Aventador is named after a fighting bull.

Huracan

The Lamborghini Huracán (Spanish for hurricane) is a sports car manufactured by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini.

Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is an SUV manufactured by Italian car manufacturer Lamborghini. The Lamborghini Urus concept was unveiled at the 2012 Beijing Auto Show on 23 April 2012.
